Description:  
    Creates a doubly linked list from input integer values then sorts them using a quicksort, prints the list before and after

Member Contributions:
    Derek Morey - 34%
    James Doherty - 33%
    Samuel Turnage - 33%

Compilation:
    $make

Sample Run:
    4 5 2 6 7 2 0
    List before sort: 
    4   5   2   6   7   2 
    List after sort: 
    2   2   4   5   6   7 

Bugs:
    None.
